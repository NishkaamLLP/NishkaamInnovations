Shipping Information

Delivery Charges:

Delivery charge varies with each Seller. Sellers incur relatively higher shipping costs on low-value items. In such cases, charging a nominal delivery charge helps them offset logistics costs. Please check your order summary to understand the delivery charges for individual products.

For Products listed as Nishkaam Plus, a Rs 40 charge for delivery per item may be applied if the order value is less than Rs 500. Orders of Rs 500 or above are delivered free.

Delivery Timeline:

The delivery date may not correspond to the delivery timeline of X-Y business days due to holidays between the order placement and delivery date, and courier partners not working on Sundays.

Estimated Delivery Time:

Sellers generally procure and ship items within the time specified on the product page. Estimated delivery time depends on factors such as the Seller, product availability, and shipping destination.

Hidden Costs:

There are NO hidden charges when you make a purchase on Nishkaam. List prices are final and all-inclusive. Delivery charges, if applicable, are clearly mentioned.

Estimated Delivery Time Variation:

The estimated delivery time varies for each seller based on product availability, geographic location of the Seller, your shipping destination, and the courier partner's time-to-deliver in your location.

Shipping Restrictions:

Sellers may not ship to certain areas due to various reasons including legal restrictions, unavailability of reliable courier partners, or Seller preference.

COD Option:

Cash on Delivery availability depends on the ability of the courier partner servicing your location to accept cash as payment at the time of delivery.

Returns:

Returns are easy. Contact Us to initiate a return. Ekart Logistics will facilitate pick-up wherever possible. Return fees are borne by the Seller.

Delivery Confirmation:

If you did not receive your order but got a delivery confirmation SMS/Email, report the issue within 7 days for investigation.

Product Availability Tags:

Tags like "In Stock", "Available", "Preorder", etc., indicate the status of the product including availability, preorder options, and more.

International Delivery:

Nishkaam currently does not deliver items internationally. Purchases can be made with credit/debit cards issued in India and 21 other countries, with delivery address in India.
